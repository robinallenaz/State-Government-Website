About Dallas

Dallas is the ninth largest city in the United States and the third largest in Texas, with a population of 1.3 million. Located in North Central Texas, Dallas is the county seat of Dallas County, with portions extending into Collin, Denton, Kaufman, and Rockwall counties. The city is part of the largest metropolitan area in the southern U.S. and is the largest inland metro area in the country without a navigable link to the sea. Dallas attracts 25.7 million annual visitors and is a major hub for business, sports, and culture. Quick Facts

  • The Dallas population is the third largest in Texas, ninth in the U.S.
  • Annual visitors: 25.7 million to Dallas, 75.5 million to the Metroplex area.
  • More than 35,000 hotel rooms are in Dallas.
  • Home to 24 Fortune 500 companies.
  • Climate: 81 rain days/year, 39.1 inches rainfall, 1.3 snow days/year, 1 inch average snowfall.
  • Notable: The frozen margarita machine, the microchip, and 7-Eleven were invented here.
