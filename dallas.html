About Dallas

Dallas is the ninth largest city in the United States and the third largest in Texas, with a population of 1.3 million. Incorporated in 1856, Dallas is located in North Central Texas and serves as the county seat of Dallas County, with portions extending into Collin, Denton, Kaufman, and Rockwall counties. The city is classified as urban and is part of the largest metropolitan area in the southern U.S. Dallas attracts 25.7 million annual visitors, is home to 24 Fortune 500 companies, and has an average household income above the Texas state average.

Sources: Visit Dallas - Fun Facts, Wikipedia - Dallas, U.S. Census Bureau
